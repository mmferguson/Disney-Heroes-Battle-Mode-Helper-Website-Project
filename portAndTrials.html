The Port

The Port offers 2 battle modes that are open different days of the week: The Docks and The Warehouse

The Docks: Open Monday, Wednesday, Friday, and Sunday. Here you can gain XP potions.

The Warehouse: Open Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday and Sunday. Here you can gain Gold.

You can win a fight 2 times each day. Chances to win reset each day at 5am local time. Only a victory drops loot and consumes a chance. There is a 10 minute cooldown after each victory before you can fight again. As the difficulty increases, so does the quality and quantity of loot dropped. Higher level difficulties are unlocked according to team level.

If you have a victory with 3 stars the chance to raid is enabled at that difficulty level. Raiding is not recommended as the port offers the chance for your heroes to gain XP without spending any stamina.

Team Trials

Team Trials offers 3 team colored battles: Red, Blue, and Yellow.

The team battles are open different days of the week. You can win a team colored fight up to 5 times each day. Chances reset each day at 5am local time.

Only a victory drops loot and consumes a chance. There is a 10 minute cooldown after each victory before you can fight again. The higher difficulty level, the more and better loot you will receive.

Raiding can be enabled after a victory with 3 stars. Raiding is not recommended as the port offers the chance for your heroes (especially the ones you might not use that much since hero selection is restricted based on team color) to gain XP without spending any stamina.
