Core Skills

Swift Strike (Normal Damage)

Dash runs across the screen punching enemies up to 7 times. Each punch does 82 damage.

Getting Punchy (Normal Damage)

Dash dashes to a random target and punches them 6 times, dealing 82 damage with each punch.

Rapid Guard

Dash uses his lightning fast speed to intercept a projectile that is about to hit an ally. Dash ignores the first 328 damage from every projectile he intercepts.

Confidence Boost

Dash gains 35 energy every time he damages an enemy of level 41 or lower. The effect is reduced against enemies above level 41.
