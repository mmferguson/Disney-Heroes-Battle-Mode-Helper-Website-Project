Core Skills

Bunny Hop (Normal Damage)

Judy jumps in the air to kick the closest enemy, knocking them backwards and doing 366 damage. The enemy's attack speed is slowed by 20% and movement speed is slowed by 20% for 3 seconds.The slow has a chance to fail against enemies higher than level 1.

Deputize

Judy inspires her whole team at the start of every wave, giving them 12.2 Basic Damage, 15% movement speed, and 15% attack speed for 7 seconds.

Testify

When an ally knocks out an enemy, Hopps heals them89 HP and inspires them for 3 seconds.The benefits of the inspire are the same as those from "Deputize"; the strength of the inspire matches the level of "Deputize".

Play Dead

The first time Judy reaches 0 HP she plays dead. A short time later, she comes back to life with 2188 HP. This miraculous trick inspires her whole team for 2 seconds. Judy is invincible for 1 second(s) after reviving.|The benefits of the inspire are the same as those from "Deputize"; the strength of the inspire matches the level of "Deputize".
