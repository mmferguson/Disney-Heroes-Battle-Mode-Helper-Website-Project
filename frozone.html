Core Skills

Stay Frosty

Frozone throws ice at foes, freezing them for 4 seconds. The freeze has a chance to only last for 1 second(s) on enemies higher than level 1.

Cold Front (Fantastic Damage)

Shoots an icy wave at the closest enemy, dealing them 192 damage.

Arctic Chill

Blasts the closest enemy, freezing them for 8 seconds. The freeze has a chance to only last for 2 seconds on enemies higher than level 21 .

Frostbite (Fantastic Damage)

Enemies that Frozone freezes take 45 damage every second they are Frozen.
