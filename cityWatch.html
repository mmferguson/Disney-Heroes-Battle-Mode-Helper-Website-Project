City Watch

City Watch consists of 15 fights across 5 regions of the city. City Watch unlocks at Team Level 25. Heroes retain their HP and energy from previous fights during a City Watch. KO'ed heroes remain KO'ed until the City Watch is reset. You earn gold after every fight and City Watch Tokens once per completed region (every 3 fights). Token rewards increase as you progress. There are two modes for City Watch: normal and hard.

Normal Mode: Gold and token rewards. No special rules

Hard Mode: Unlocks after a normal City Watch has been completed (all 15). Disk Power (a key resource for upgrading your heroes' Memory Disks) and +50% gold rewards. HP and energy don't regenerate.

*Make sure that your team has a good healer with leveled up healing skills. Make sure that you have a strong tank that can last multiple battles (you can also double up and use 2 tanks). Don't always accept the success of a battle, you can rerun the fight as many times as you want so experiment with different hero combos to find the best result. Heroes save their energy between fights so use a variety of heroes in the beginning fights so you can use their skills in later regions when battles get tough.*
