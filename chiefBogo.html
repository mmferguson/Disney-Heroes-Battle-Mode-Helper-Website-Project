Core Skills

Serve and Protect

Grants himself a shield that blocks 774 damage. The shield expires after 5 seconds.

Criminal Charge (Normal Damage)

At the start of combat, Chief Bogo charges at the frontmost enemy, dealing 193 damage and stunning them for 5 seconds. The Stun has a chance to fail against enemies higher than level 1.

Blinding Justice (Fantastic Damage)

Throws a flashbang grenade dealing 179damage to enemies hit and blinding them for 5 seconds. The blind has a chance to fail against enemies higher than level 21 damage each.

Heal Up

Chief Bogo now heals 175HP every time his "Serve and Protect" shield takes damage.
