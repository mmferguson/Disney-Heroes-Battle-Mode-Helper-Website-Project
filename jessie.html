Core Skills

Hootenanny (Normal Damamge)

Jessie leaps into the middle of the enemies and kicks them repeateadly, doing 82 with each kick.

Sonic Yodel (Fantastic Damage)

Jessie lets out an ear piercing yodel that damages all enemies. The yodel does at least 62. The damage increases the closer Jessie is to the enemies, up to a max of 367

Cowgirl Boogie

Jessie's cowgirl boots now heal her every time she damages an enemy. She gains 45% of damage done back as healing against targets 21 and below. The amount of healing received from this skill is reduced against enemies who are above level 21.

Kick Up a Row

Jessie's line dancing skills allow her to dodge enemy attacks. She has a 25% chance to dodge any basic attacks or skills from enemies level 41 or below. Jessie's chance to dodge is reduced against enemies who are above level 41.
