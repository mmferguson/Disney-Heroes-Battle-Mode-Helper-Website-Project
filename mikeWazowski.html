Core Skills

Sonic Belch (Fantastic Damage)

Mike eats his microphone and then belches, doing 225 damage to all enemies.

Canister Toss

Mike throws a scream canister at the closest enemy, scaring them and all nearby enemies for 8 seconds. Attacks that do Fantastic damage always crit against Scared enemies. The Scare has a chance fail against enemies above level 1.

On a Roll (Fantastic Damage)

Mike rolls across the battlefield dealing 134 damage to all enemies.

Fight or Fright

When Mike damages a scared enemy, he also stuns them for 3 seconds. The stun has a chance to fail against enemies above level 41.
