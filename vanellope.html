Core Skills

Lollipop Slammer (Fantastic Damage)

Uses her glitch to become untargetable and teleport into the middle of her enemies. She slams her candy hammer, dealing 195 damage to all enemies near her, then teleports back behind her allies.

Soda Geyser (Fantastic Damage)

Combines the power of mint and soda to shoot a massive spray of soda at her closest enemy, dealing 189 damage. The corn syrup slows their movement speed by 25% and their attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds.

Minty Fresh (Fantastic Damage)

Launches a minty cannon ball at the furthest enemy, dealing 353 damage.

Sharing is Caring

All of Vanellope's basic attacks, "Soda Geyser" and "Minty Fresh" gain 135 splash damage.
