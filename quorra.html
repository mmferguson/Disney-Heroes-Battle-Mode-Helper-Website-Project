Core Skills

Disc Spin (Fantastic Damage)

Quorra attacks nearby enemies with her light disc and sword, dealing 45 damage with each attack. Quorra attacks 7 times.

Light Cycle (Fantastic Damage)

At the start of combat, Quorra rides her lightbike past the enemies dealing 191 damage to each enemy. Quorra is untargetable while riding her lightbike.

Disc Ricochet (Fantastic Damage)

Quorra throws her light disc at the furthest enemy dealing 462 damage. It then bounces, hitting up to two other enemies for 249 damage each.

Grid Power

Each hit of "Disc Spin" does bonus damage equal to 10% of her target's Max HP. This effect is reduced against targets above level 41.
