Normal Campaign

The Normal Campaign is the main route of progression in the game. Levels must first be completed in the Normal Campaign in order to unlock the corresponding levels in the Elite and Friend Campaigns.

Here you'll follow the game's story while earning badges that can be used to promote your heroes.

Each level in Chapters 1-6 costs 6 stamina. Each level in Chapters 7-10 costs 10 stamina.

Raiding

After completing a level with 3 stars the ability to raid that level is enabled.

There are 3 options when raiding: Raid 1, Raid 10, and Raid MAX. Raid 10 and Raid MAX are only available to players with a level 4 VIP status.

Raiding autocompletes the battle so your heroes don't have to fight. You automatically receive your rewards (coins, badges, XP Drinks, and Team XP).

*Your heroes do NOT gain Hero XP and stamina is used*

Infection

>

All levels in the Normal Campaign are susceptible to infection.

When a level is infected the enemies are strengthened depending on the power of your own heroes

After defeating an infected level you receive disk power along with the usual rewards for that level. Disk power is used to level up memory disks obtained through hero friendships.

Infections appear randomly

Elite Campaign

The elite is where you can earn hero chips to evolve your heroes.The Elite campaign levels are harder than the Normal campaign levels so make sure your heroes are strong enough to complete the level.

Each chapter features one hero by providing 3 levels for that one hero while the other 4 levels are different heroes (e.g. Chapter 2 features 3 Finnick levels and 1 level each for Ralph, Frozone, Vanellope, and Elastigirl)

The early chapter levels have a 12 stamina cost per level run. The later chapter levels have a 16 stamina cost per level run.

Each Elite campaign level has a total of 3 runs per day. There is the ability to reset the level runs given VIP status or Team Level using diamonds

Friend Campaign

The Friends feature unlocks at Team Level 30. Some hero friendships require a higher team level. A Friend campaign unlocks when a friendship reaches level 3.

Each Friend campaign tells a story about the relationship between the two heroes. Both heroes are required for every battle. As the friend campaign progresses a few additional heroes are allowed to join the battles.

Unlike Normal and Elite campaigns, Friend campaigns use friend stamina. Each chapter contains 7 levels each with an episode consisting of 5 battles.

Missions

Missions are quests that you can send pairs of friends on that bring back rewards. Each mission takes a fixed time to complete and has a chance of success. If the mission doesn't succeed you receive the badges you put forth to start the mission. As the friend level increases, more missions will be unlocked. Higher friend-level missions have better rewards but take more time. Completing a mission several times can increase the level of the mission and increase the reward.

Lower friend-level missions reward Friend XP. Higher friend-level missions also award disk power and memories (2 key ingredients in upgrading Memory Disks)

Memory Disks

>

Memory Disks are special items that heroes can equip and upgrade. Each disk is designed to enhance key traits of the hero who equips it. Whenever a friend campaign is completed, a memory disk will be unlocked for the primary hero in the friendship. A hero can only equip one memory disk at a time. A memory disk has a level and a star rating.

Higher disk levels increase the bonus to one of the hero's key stats. Upgrading a disk level requires disk power. Disk power is earned by completing missions, clearing infected levels in the normal campaign, and completing stages in City Watch hard mode.

Additional stars increase the power of whatever unique ability the disk provides the hero. Upgrading star ratings requires memories. Memories can be earned by completing missions.
