Core Skills

Ion Blaster (Fantastic Damamge)

Zurg unleashes his ion blaster and fires it repeatedly until he runs out of energy. Each shot from the ion blaster deals 71 damage to each enemy.

Zurg Fury

Zurg shouts, scaring enemies for 7 seconds. Attacks that do Fantastic damage always crit against Scared enemies. This has a chance to fail against enemies above level 1.

Energy Heist

Zurg steals 60 energy from each enemy. This has a chance to fail against enemies above 21.

Evil Genius

Zurg gains 20 energy every time he damages, scares, or steals energy from an enemy of level 41 or lower.This effect is reduced against enemies above level 41.
