Core Skills

Scan The Horizon

Barbossa pulls out his spyglass and Studies all enemies in front of him. Enemies remain Studied for 6 seconds. Barbossa also gains 400% increased attack speed for 6 seconds. Attacks that do Normal damage always crit against Studied enemies. | The Study has a chance to fail against enemies above level 1.

Apple of Me Eye

Barbossa eats an apple, healing 224 HP and gaining 250 energy.

Chain Shot (Normal Damage)

During his Basic Attack, Barbossa replaces every 3rd cannonball with a chain shot that does 359 damage and slows the target's attack speed by 90% for 1 seconds. The slow has a chance to fail against enemies higher than level 21.

Hot Shot (Normal Damage)

During his Basic Attack, Barbossa's normal cannonballs are now heated, causing them to do 728 damage over 3 seconds.
