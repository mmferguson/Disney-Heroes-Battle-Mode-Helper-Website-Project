Core Skills

Secure Squad

Violet jumps out of danger and shields her entire team, protecting them from 244 damage for the next 7 seconds.

Invisibility

Before Violet is about to take damage, she becomes invisible for 10 seconds. While invisible, Violet is untargetable. Violet can only become invisible once every 25 seconds. When damaged by enemies higher than level 1 there is a chance Violet is unable to turn invisible. Also, this skill will not trigger when Violet has a shield.

Shield Roll (Fantastic Damage)

Violet bowls into her enemies with her force field. Enemies hit take 125 damage and are knocked back.

Force Field

Violet shields her weakest ally, protecting them from 1368 damage for the next 7 seconds.
