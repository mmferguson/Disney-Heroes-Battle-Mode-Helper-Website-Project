Arena

The Arena allows you to fight other players on your server. The Arena unlocks at Team Level 10.

There are 6 tiers: Copper, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Challengers

Copper, Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum each have 5 divisions: I, II, III, IV, V

Top Rewards: 500 Diamonds, 100,000 Gold, 800 Arena Tokens, 3 Mega XP Drinks

To be promoted to a higher division you must stay in the top 5 long enough. The higher your rank the greater the reward.

Challengers

To fight a Challenger, you must also be a Challenger or be ranked 1st in Platinum I.

To promote to a Challenger you must defeat a Challenger (There is no promotion timer in Platinum I).

There is no demotion timer in Challenger, players demote back to 1st in Platinum once they drop below the minimum Challenger rank.

Challengers will only appear above you in Platinum I while you are ranked 1st in Platinum I.

Guilds

Guilds are available at Team Level 15. Guilds allow you team up with other players at a max group capacity of 50 players. Together you and your guild can reap rewards and fight the Creep Surge every day. It is highly recommended to join a guild as soon as possible, especially an active one.

Guild Chat: There is a special chat channel just for the members of your guild to communicate of team comps and the daily Creep Surge.

Check-In

Earn rewards as you and your guild members check each day

Check-In rewards: 1,000 Guild Influence, 25 Guild Coins, 10,000 Gold, 2 Mega XP Drinks

Perks

There are several guild rewards that can be bought using Guild Influence Points. Some rewards are also given based on the Guild's level and check-in rate

Earn Guild Influence by spending stamina in the Campaign, completing Daily Quests, checking into the Guild Check-In and battling in the Creep Surge.

Every time a member makes an In App Purchase (IAP), everyone in the Guild receives Guild Coins (Bigger purchases award more coins).

*Only Guild Leaders and Champions can spend Influence to unlock or upgrade various Guild Perks*

Mercenaries

You can hire heroes from guild members to help you defeat regions in the Creep Surge and City Watch with gold (You are only able to hire one mercenary each day for City Watch so choose wisely). You can post heroes to the Merc Board so other members in your guild can hire your heroes.

Only heroes level 20 and up can be posted. Each mercenary poseted can be hired once by one guild member. Hired or not, that mercenary slot remains filled for 20 hours.

If your mercenary is hired you earn 100 guild coins. Unhired mercenaries earn 30 guild coins.

Mercenary slots are unlocked by upgrading Guild Perks (There are 5 slots).

Crates

Earn guild coins that you can use to buy Guild Crates (250 for 1 and 1,125 for 5). You get one free Guild Crate every day. The Guild Crate has a featured hero that cannot be obtained in any other crates.

Buy 1 Guild Crate

Hero Chips: 20%

Credit: 2% (Silver, gold, or platinum)

XP Drinks: 1.5% (Mini or grande)

Buy 5 Guild Crates

Badges: 73.2% (Full white, green, and blue badges, or purple bits)

Creep Surge

Work together with your guild members to defeat as many waves of Creeps as possible.

Make sure to contribute at least one attack to the Surge each day. You'll earn participation rewards for the Surge and for your Daily Quest. Participants earn Surge Tokens to spend in the Surge Shop.

Each hero can fight only once per day. *Unlocking and upgrading your heroes will allow you more opportunities to earn more Gold rewards and clear districts*

You can hire one Mercenary per attack in the Creep Surge (You must have a hero to join the mercenary). Hiring a mercenary costs gold.

Surges run from 8am to 12am in your guild time zone. Guild leaders can change this time zone.

Guild officers can set Surge difficulty in the guild settings. Increasing difficulty increases both the rewards and the waves needed to be cleared. Surge difficulty sets the number of waves the guild needs to clear (3 to 15). Each wave requires clearing any 4 of the 6 regions in the city. Clearing a region means defeating all the creeps in each of its districts (Some regions have more districts than others).

At the end of the Surge, everyone in the guild who participated gets 10 Surge Tokens for each region cleared. The guild receives 1000 Influence for each region cleared. If the guild clears ALL waves before the time runs out, everyone who participated gets an extra 30 Tokens. The guild also gets and extra 5000 Influence.

The gold star identifies a creep lineup that makes the most sense to attack (based on which regions your guild is clearing and which heroes you have remaining).

However, if your guild is still in early development, attack the lower level regions to ensure you complete the Surge. You don't need to use a complete team, less is more - this will allow you to stretch the utility of all heroes and clear more districts.

Guild Ranks

Promote members inside your guild to give them status and power, but be careful who you promote. Officers and Champions can kick players and take leadership from an inactive leader. Tap any row in any member list to promote or demote people.

Ranks

Member

All new people start at the Member rank

Veteran

Rank of prestige, but no additional power

Officer

Can claim leadership if the leader is inactive for 3 weeks

Can promote and demote members of lower ranks

Can kick lower ranked members

Can approve/deny new member requests

Champion

Can claim leadership if the leader is inactive for 1 week

Can promote and demote members of lower ranks

Can kick lower ranked members

Can approve/deny new member requests

Can edit the guild's emblem

Can post to, edit, and delete posts on the guild wall

Can edit the difficulty of the Creep Surge

Leader

Can edit all guild settings

Can make another player the Leader

*There can only be one Leader at a time

Coliseum

The Coliseum allows you to fight other players (similar to the Arena). But, you can challenge the strength of another player's heroes in 3 teams of 5 heroes (15 heroes max for a coliseum fight). The Coliseum unlocks at Team Level 44.

Similar to the Arena, the Coliseum has 6 tiers: Copper, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Challengers

Copper, Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum each have 5 divisions: I, II, III, IV, V

Top Rewards: 500 Diamonds, 800 Coliseum Tokens, 5 Turbo XP Drinks

To be promoted to a higher division you must stay in the top 5 long enough. The higher your rank the greater the reward.

Challengers

To fight a Challenger, you must also be a Challenger or be ranked 1st in Platinum I.

To promote to a Challenger you must defeat a Challenger (There is no promotion timer in Platinum I).

There is no demotion timer in Challenger, players demote back to 1st in Platinum once they drop below the minimum Challenger rank.

Challengers will only appear above you in Platinum I while you are ranked 1st in Platinum I.

Markets

Market

The Market unlocks after you complete Chapter 2 in the Normal Campaign.

Here you can use your gold and diamonds on XP Drinks, Hero Chips, Badges, and Bits.

Arena Shop

The Arena Shop unlocks at Team Level 10 along with the Arena.

Here you can spend your Arena Tokens on Credits, Hero Chips (500 Arena Tokens for 5 Hero Chips), Badges, and Bits.

Surge Shop

The Surge Shop unlocks at Team Level 30 along with the Creep Surge.

Here you can spend your Surge Tokens on XP Drinks, Hero Chips (500 Surge Tokens for 5 Hero Chips), Badges, and Bits.

City Watch Shop

The City Watch Shop unlocks at Team Level 25 along with City Watch.

Here you can spend your City Watch Tokens on Credits, Hero Chips (500 City Watch Tokens for 5 Hero Chips), Badges, and Bits.

Coliseum Shop

The Coliseum Shop unlocks at Team Level 44 along with the Coliseum.

Here you can spend your Coliseum Tokens on XP Drinks, Hero Chips (500 Coliseum Tokens for 5 Hero Chips), Badges, and Bits.

Black Market

The Black Market can be found occasionally when raiding after Team Level 31.

Here you can spend your gold and diamonds on Hero Chips, XP Drinks, Credits, Badges, and Bits.

Mega Mart

The Mega Mart can be found rarely when raiding after Team Level 41.

Here you can spend your gold and diamonds on Hero Chips, XP Drinks, Credits, Badges, Bits, Memories, and Disk Power.
