Core Skills

Lemming Rush (Fantastic Damamge)

Nick summons a business lemming with a pawpsicle who deal 92 damage to all enemies. Each time Nick uses this ability, an additional lemming is summoned, up to a max of 5. Each extra lemming does 31 damage to all enemies. Nick keeps his lemmings between waves of a fight.

Shuriken Pops (Fantastic Damage)

Nick pulls out some stashed pawpsicles and flings three of them at the closest enemies dealing 134 damage with each hit.

Charming Fox

Nick uses his skills of persuasion to charm the closest enemy to fight on his side for 7 seconds. While charmed, the target's basic attacks do 43 bonus damage.

Proper Reinforcements

Nick gains 219 skill power for every ally left standing, including charmed opponents. He also gains 219 skill power for every lemming his "Lemming Rush" has accumulated.
